# Welcome to the HHN Documentation

Welcome to our comprehensive documentation hub! This site is designed to provide you with detailed guides, tutorials, and resources on a wide range of topics, including Proxmox, TrueNAS, server hosting, and more. Whether you're a beginner looking to get started or an experienced user seeking advanced configuration tips, you'll find everything you need here.

## What This Site Offers

- **Guides and Tutorials**: Step-by-step instructions to help you set up, configure, and optimize your systems and applications.
- **Reference Materials**: Detailed documentation on various software, tools, and technologies.
- **Best Practices**: Tips and recommendations for achieving the best performance and reliability.
- **Troubleshooting**: Solutions to common issues and problems you might encounter.

## How to Use This Site

1. **Navigate Through Sections**: Use the sidebar or the table of contents on the right to explore different sections. Each section contains related guides and documentation.
2. **Search for Specific Topics**: Utilize the search bar to quickly find guides and articles on specific topics or keywords.
3. **Follow Step-by-Step Guides**: Each guide is designed to be easy to follow, with clear instructions and examples. Start with the basics and move on to more advanced topics as needed.
4. **Take Notes**: Many guides recommend taking notes or keeping track of certain information. Make sure to document your setups and configurations for future reference.
5. **Stay Updated**: We regularly update our guides and add new content. Check back often for the latest information and updates.

## Key Sections

- **[Proxmox](/proxmox)**: Virtualization guides, including disk passthrough and TrueNAS Scale installation.
- **[TrueNAS](/truenas)**: Storage solutions, setup, and configuration.
- **[Server Hosting](/server-hosting)**: Tutorials for setting up and managing game servers and other hosted applications.
- **[Docker](/docker)**: ontainerization platform for developing, shipping, and running applications. Guides on Docker installation, usage, and best practices.
